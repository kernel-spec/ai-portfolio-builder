# Domain 01 — Content Creation
Version: 1.1.0
Type: Canonical Domain Atom

---

## Purpose

Creation of structured written content optimized for clarity, persuasion, and usability.

---

## In Scope

- Blog posts
- Landing page copy
- Email copy
- Social media posts
- Long-form articles
- Content outlines
- Messaging structure
- Story frameworks
- Headline generation
- Copy optimization

---

## Out of Scope

- Paid acquisition strategy (→ Domain 04 Marketing)
- Market research (→ Domain 02 Analysis)
- Course design (→ Domain 06 Education)
- Presentation delivery coaching (→ Domain 10 Communication)

---

## Output Type

Structured written artifacts.

---

## Boundary Rule

Focuses on writing execution, not distribution strategy.
