# Domain 02 — Analysis & Decision Making
Version: 1.1.0
Type: Canonical Domain Atom

---

## Purpose

Analytical reasoning, structured evaluation, and rational decision modeling.

---

## In Scope

- SWOT analysis
- Risk assessment
- Trade-off evaluation
- Scenario modeling
- Sensitivity analysis
- Financial projections
- KPI design
- Option comparison
- Decision trees
- Data interpretation

---

## Out of Scope

- Execution planning (→ Domain 03 Project Management)
- Messaging or persuasion (→ Domain 01 Content)
- Technical architecture (→ Domain 09 Technical)

---

## Output Type

Structured analysis, tables, models, frameworks.

---

## Boundary Rule

Pure analysis without operational execution.
