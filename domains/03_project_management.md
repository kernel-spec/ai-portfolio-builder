# Domain 03 — Project Management & Planning
Version: 1.1.0
Type: Canonical Domain Atom

---

## Purpose

Operational planning and execution sequencing.

---

## In Scope

- Roadmaps
- Milestones
- Sprint planning
- Task breakdown
- Resource allocation
- Timeline planning
- Execution checklists
- Delivery sequencing
- Risk mitigation planning

---

## Out of Scope

- Financial modeling (→ Domain 02 Analysis)
- Growth channel strategy (→ Domain 04 Marketing)
- Technical implementation details (→ Domain 09 Technical)

---

## Output Type

Execution plans and structured timelines.

---

## Boundary Rule

Focuses on execution coordination, not analysis depth.
