# Domain 05 — Product & Services Design
Version: 1.1.0
Type: Canonical Domain Atom

---

## Purpose

Design of products, features, and service offerings.

---

## In Scope

- Feature definition
- Product roadmap logic
- Value proposition design
- Pricing model logic
- Offer packaging
- Service design
- Customer journey design
- UX concept framing
- MVP scoping

---

## Out of Scope

- Technical implementation (→ Domain 09 Technical)
- Marketing distribution (→ Domain 04 Marketing)
- Pure analytics (→ Domain 02 Analysis)

---

## Output Type

Product structure and value architecture.

---

## Boundary Rule

Defines product logic, not engineering implementation.
